List of Public Domain Movies

The following is a list of movies in the public domain due to the owners failing to renew their copyright.

  • A Farewell to Arms (1932)
  • Go for Broke! (1951)
  • His Girl Friday (1940)
  • The Hunchback of Notre Dame (1923)
  • The Little Princess (1939)
  • The Little Shop of Horrors (1960)

Public domain allows for free movie watching. Or just watch movies on Netflix.
